MeOutlet is the safest power outlet yet invented. We put safety first yet are driven by design thinking. No electrical contacts mean there is no chance for electricity-related incidents. The socket is created based on the magnetic resonance principle and is the safest version of the device there is. The magnetic qualities also allow for the elevated levels of convenience while using the power outlet: helping to easily connect the socket and the plug: be it in the dark, hard-to-reach places or at an angle.
MePower
Pre-order
MePower cable-free charging device is a next generation of resonance charging systems. When installed, MePower charging surface turns any surface into a charging surface. The application is extensive, as it can be used to power household electronic devices & wearables, like a laptop, a phone, a smartwatch; or used outside to charge drones or even electric cars. The technology allows for multiple devices to be charged at the same time. There is no need for cables, plugs and adapters. The system supports 6kWt and 11kWt electric vehicle charging. The charging occurs upon contact or at a distance.
